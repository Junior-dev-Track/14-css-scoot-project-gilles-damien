FAQs

How it works?

How do I download the app?

To download the Scoot app, you can search “Scoot” in both the App and Google Play stores. An even simpler way to do it would be to click the relevant link at the bottom of this page and you’ll be re-directed to the correct page.

Can I find a nearby Scoots?

Yes, you can! The Scoot app will show you all the available scooters in your area. You can also use the app to reserve a scooter for up to 30 minutes before you pick it up.

Do I need a license to ride?

Yes, you do. You need a valid driver’s license to ride a Scoot. If you’re riding a scooter in a country where you’re required to wear a helmet, you’ll need to wear one while riding a Scoot.

Safe driving

Should I wear a helmet?

Yes, please do! All cities have different laws. But we strongly strongly strongly recommend always wearing a helmet regardless of the local laws. We like you and we want you to be as safe as possible while Scooting.

How about the rules & regulations?

We want you to be safe and legal. We expect all riders to follow local laws and rules of the road. This includes things like stopping at red lights and stop signs, yielding to pedestrians, and not riding on sidewalks.
